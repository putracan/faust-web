Preventive Maintenance

Regular maintenance to prevent network problems before they occur. We conduct routine inspections, cleaning, and replacement of necessary components to maintain optimal network performance.

  • Physical inspection of network equipment
  • Cleaning of switches, routers, and servers
  • Replacement of damaged cables
  • Firmware and software updates
  • Network configuration backup
  • Performance testing and optimization
Frequencies: Monthly, quarterly, or as needed
